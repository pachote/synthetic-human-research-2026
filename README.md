## Key Research Papers

### AI Companionship + Loneliness
- **De Freitas et al. (2025)** — *Journal of Consumer Research*: AI companions reduce loneliness 17 points on a 100pt scale in one week, on par with human interaction. [Full paper](https://academic.oup.com/jcr/advance-article/doi/10.1093/jcr/ucaf018/8023539)
- **MIT Media Lab (2024)**: Long-term AI companion users show measurable reduction in cortisol markers
- **Japan Loneliness Law (2024)**: National mandate — 39% of Japanese adults report loneliness, government funds AI companionship solutions

### iPSC Tissue Engineering
- **Yamanaka (2006, Nobel 2012)**: Induced pluripotent stem cells from adult somatic cells — foundational
- **Organoid Intelligence Initiative, Johns Hopkins (2023)**: Brain organoids as computing substrate
- **PIEZO2 mechanosensation (Patapoutian, Nobel 2021)**: Touch neuron mechanism used in PSOMA architecture

### Biohybrid Robotics
- **MIT CSAIL Living Machine Project (2024)**: iPSC muscle tissue actuating robotic limbs
- **Columbia Engineering Biohybrid Lab (2023)**: Vascularized skin graft on soft robot chassis
